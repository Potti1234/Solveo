How it works

Four agents, one queue for your managers.

Each agent handles a different channel where guest feedback shows up. Everything that needs a human judgment call lands in one approval queue — nothing gets sent to a guest without a manager's sign-off.

Voice Agent

Answers incoming calls, understands the request, and routes anything time-sensitive straight to housekeeping or the front desk.

Retrieval Agent

Watches review sites and inboxes, and surfaces new guest feedback the moment it's posted, positive or negative.

Scheduling Agent

Flags booking conflicts and service delays before they reach the guest, and proposes a fix for the manager to confirm.

Manager Console

Every proposed compensation or reply lands here first — a single scrollable queue your team can approve or decline.

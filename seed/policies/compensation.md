# Guest Compensation Policy

## Clause §4.1 - Minor Amenity Interruption
When a listed amenity is unavailable for less than two waking hours and staff can provide a reasonable substitute, guest relations may offer points, a snack credit, or up to 10% of the affected nightly rate.

## Clause §4.2 - Essential Comfort Failure Overnight
When heat, air conditioning, plumbing, electricity, or door access fails for at least four overnight hours and hotel logs corroborate the issue, guest relations may approve a 30% refund of the affected stay value without manager approval. The response must cite the booking record, the maintenance record, and this clause.

## Clause §4.3 - Full Refund Threshold
A full refund requires either an uninhabitable room, documented relocation failure, or a manager override. A demand alone is not evidence for a full refund.

## Clause §4.4 - Goodwill Limit
Goodwill credits over USD 75 require a supervisor note unless another numbered clause authorizes the amount.

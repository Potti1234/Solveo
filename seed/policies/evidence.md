# Evidence and Claim Review Policy

## Clause §2.1 - Corroborated Records
Claims are considered corroborated when at least two independent sources align, such as a booking record plus a maintenance log, a housekeeping log plus a timestamped image, or a policy clause plus a staff note.

## Clause §2.2 - Photo Evidence
Photo evidence must be interpreted narrowly. Guest relations may rely on visible conditions only; do not infer mold, pests, or filth unless the image or staff record supports that specific condition.

## Clause §2.3 - Same-Day Cleaning Records
If housekeeping records show a room was cleaned and inspected on the same morning as a complaint, later cleanliness claims require stronger evidence before compensation is approved.

## Clause §2.4 - Repeat Refund Context
Prior refund behavior is never the sole basis for denial. It may be cited as escalation context when current evidence does not substantiate the claim.

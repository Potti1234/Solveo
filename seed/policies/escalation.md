# Escalation and Guest Relations Policy

## Clause §7.1 - Safety or Health Escalation
Claims involving safety, health, mold, pests, smoke, bodily fluids, or discriminatory treatment must be escalated if evidence is ambiguous or if the guest posts publicly.

## Clause §7.2 - Serial Refund Pattern
If a guest has three or more prior refunds and the current claim is unsubstantiated, guest relations should decline compensation politely and escalate the evidence bundle for manager review.

## Clause §7.3 - Public Review Tone
Responses to public reviews must acknowledge the concern, avoid accusing the guest, cite that the team reviewed records, and invite direct follow-up.

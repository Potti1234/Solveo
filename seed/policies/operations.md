# Operations Pattern Policy

## Clause §6.1 - Repeated Issue Alert
When three or more open or recent tickets share an issue type and location within seven days, create a severity-ranked operations alert for the department lead.

## Clause §6.2 - Guest Impact Priority
Issues affecting occupied rooms outrank back-of-house work unless a safety hazard is present.

## Clause §6.3 - Maintenance Ticket From Claim
When a claim is legitimate or partially legitimate and involves a physical room condition, create or update a maintenance ticket even when compensation is already approved.
